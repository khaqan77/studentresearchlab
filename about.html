About Us

It’s where students can get help to solve their projects and find new projects looking for help. Knowledge sharing. Find you project of interest and enhance your skills. Hands on experience. Benefitting from each other’s knowledge. Students can put their research here and companies can come and buy. Or companies can ask students to perform research for them. Share your research and get rewarded. Ideas which can change the world. Students will be working on organizational research projects free of charge to get recognition. Organizational projects (on which students work) can be counted against course work/credit hours. Organizations can post a problem and students can find/research a solution. (Students don’t have resources to build product/services)
